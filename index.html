// Philosophy

The hardest question in technology isn't "How do we build this?"
It's "Should we?"

We help you figure out what's actually worth building before you commit your time, money, or team.

Sometimes the answer is "not yet." Sometimes it's "this, but differently." And sometimes the most valuable thing we can tell you is that it's a bad idea - and we'll prove it.
